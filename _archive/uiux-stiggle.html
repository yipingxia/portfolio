STIGGLE: Worry Projects No More!

A smart group project manager app.

The project was the final project for a module entitled "TR3001: New Product Development".
I had the opportunity to collaborate with three busniess students from both Singapore and Sweden, and we proposed this idea of a smart project manager app for better college student project management.
We came up with a complete busniess plan for the development of the application.
It was a very interesting experience again, however, it was a pity when we were able to continue develop the product when the semester finished.
